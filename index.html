01 Wonder World Portal CRM / Customer Portal 2024 →

All-in-one CRM / customer portal system

An all-in-one CRM and customer portal that organized Wonder World’s workflow and helped increase conversions by 27%.

Constraint

Wonder World’s workflow was spread across customers, quotes, admin tasks, product details, and follow-ups. Their team needed one organized place to manage each customer and each project without losing context.

Built

We built an all-in-one CRM and customer portal system. Each customer gets their own portal, while admins can view customer details, project status, quote activity, and workflow updates from one clean operating layer.

Outcome

The buying path became clearer, the admin side became more organized, and the improved workflow helped boost conversions by 27%.
